connection

Connection to the SDG

Marine biologist Sylvia Earle explains that every breath we take and every drop of water we drink connects us to the ocean. However, plastic pollution continues to harm marine animals and their habitats. Protecting Life Below Water means protecting these ecosystems and the species that live in them. Simple choices, like using less single-use plastic, can help keep our oceans healthy.

Skip one plastic, let wild seas thrive.
